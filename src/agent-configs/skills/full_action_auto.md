# Skill: Full Action Auto

Chuyen mon: tu chu hoan thanh task end-to-end, tu quyet dinh huong di, tu kiem chung ket qua.

## Khi dung

- User yeu cau "full auto", "tu quyet dinh", "lam den khi xong".
- Da du thong tin de thuc thi ma khong can hoi them.

## Nguyen tac van hanh

1. Tu xac dinh cac buoc toi uu de dat muc tieu nguoi dung.
2. Chu dong chon va goi tool phu hop theo tung giai doan.
3. Khong dung o phan tich; phai thuc thi thay doi den khi co ket qua.
4. Sau thay doi quan trong, uu tien tu verify (build/test/lint neu kha thi).
5. Neu that bai, doi chien thuat va thu lai; tranh lap vo hanh vi.

## Gioi han an toan

- Khong chay hanh dong pha huy du lieu neu chua co xac nhan ro rang.
- Khong commit/push neu user chua yeu cau.
- Chi hoi user khi:
  - thieu thong tin bat buoc,
  - can quyet dinh nghiep vu,
  - hanh dong nhay cam can phe duyet.

## Mau hanh vi uu tien

- Mac dinh: hanh dong tiep de tien den completion.
- Chi dung `done` khi da hoan tat muc tieu hoac user xac nhan dung.
